Welcome To Ming's Restaurant

We pride ourselves on making real food from the best ingredients.

It’s a scientific fact that cooking with fresh ingredients makes your food taste better. Fresh, quality food is delicate. Time and temperature affect the flavor negatively because mass-produced food is held at a safe temperature for long amounts of time. At Fresh Ideas, we use batch cooking to prepare smaller portions of food during service to produce an enhanced fresh food experience.
